FIZZBUZZ CHALLENGE

Here is a Challenge that prints all numbers from 1 to 100 with two exceptions. For number divisible by 3, it prints "Fizz" instead of the number, and for numbers divisible by 5 (and not 3), it prints "Buzz" instead of the number and then the challenge is modified to print "FizzBuzz" for numbers that are divisible by both 3 and 5 (and still prints "Fizz" and "Buzz" for numbers divisible by only one of those)

You can view the result by doing the following

  • Right Click on this page
  • Click on the inspect option. A taskbar will pop up on either side of the browser. The taskbar has a menu bar with the the following options; Elements, Console, Sources, Network, etc
  • Click on the Console option to view the result of our FizzBuzz challenge
